Chicken Stir Fry

Description

Chicken stir fry is a relatively cheap and tasty recipe. It combines rice, flavored chicken, and vegetables together to create a dish which is both filling and plentiful for consumers on a budget.

Ingredients

  • Diced Chicken
  • Assorted Vegetables
  • White or Brown Rice

Steps

  1. Cook rice and bring to a boil, save for later
  2. Cut up vegetables and cook over low heat
  3. Dice and cook chicken
  4. Combine all ingredients to one pan and cook, adding stir fry sauce for taste
  5. Serve in bowls, can be save for leftovers
