Lasagna

Description

Lasagana is a type of italian dish made from combining flat sheets of pasta together with assorted cheeses such as ricotta and mozzarella and cobining it with cheese sauce. The dish may then be topped of with spices such as garlic and oregano, and grated cheese is usually added on top. It is then baked in an oven and served in square sized portions.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Meats (optional)

Steps

  1. Mix sauces with spices over medium heat
  2. Add sauce over prepared lasagna noodles and repeat process 2 or 3 times until it gains height
  3. Bake dish in oven at 350 degrees for about 45 minutes or until a golden brown
  4. Cut into sizable squares and serve
